Who we are and what we do

At the Digital Skills Organisation (DSO) our priority is making sure that digital skills training in Australia produces job-ready candidates with the skills that employers need.

Innovative training

We develop fresh, innovative ways of training people in digital skills.

Accessibility

We make world-leading digital training more accessible.

Relevance

We are working towards creating a training system that is fast, relevant and responsive to what industry needs.

Impact

We curate and initiate impactful projects and initiatives in the digital skills sector

Our work is designed to help meet the digital workforce needs of tomorrow.

Digital Skills Experiments -
Pilot Project August 2020

The first of our Pilots is about to take place, and we’d like to invite you to take part.
The initial stages of the Pilot will require a time commitment of 4.5 hours and will involve:

24th August – 90-minute online video conference for all participants

  • Introduction to DSO
  • How the workshop will work
  • Setting the challenge topics

25th-27th August – two-hour one-on-one discussion with DSO
28th August – 60-minute online video conference for all participants

  • Summary of workshop results
  • Next steps
